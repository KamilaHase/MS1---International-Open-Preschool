Are you looking for tips what to do and where to go in Gothenburg? Here we gathered a few links of places that we hope might help you to get to know the city better. For more information please visit the home pages of each institution.

  • Öppen Förskola in Gothenburg - list of regular open preschools
  • Världskulturmuseet - Museum of World culture, 2nd floor dedicated to children, free entrance
  • Universeum - a science centre and museum dedicated to children
  • Liseberg - amusement park
  • Göteborgs stadsmuseum - Gothenburg City Museum, 3rd floor dedicated to children
  • Göteborgs Naturhistoriska Museum - Gothenburg Museum of Natural History, free entrance
  • Leo's Lekland - fun park, trampolines, slides etc., two premises in Gothenburg: Mölndal and Partille
  • Outdoor playgrounds - list of most playgrounds around town
